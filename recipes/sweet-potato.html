Sweet Potato Mash

Description

This is a savory dish for smooth and creamy sweet potatoes without all the sugar. (Makes enough for 5 people

Ingredients

  • 6 Sweet potatoes
  • 250ml Soured cream
  • Salt and Pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. Peel Potatoes and cut up into cubes about 4cm thick
  2. Boil Potatoes for 15 mins
  3. Drain and mash Potatoes, adding the sour cream and salt and pepper for taste
